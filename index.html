01

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Profile information and social media handles

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our services, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address, browser type and version, device information, operating system

  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, date and time of visits

We may receive information from social media platforms, authentication providers, advertising partners, analytics providers, business partners, and publicly available sources.

We use information to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
